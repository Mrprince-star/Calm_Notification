Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

Introduction

Calm Notifications is a free Android app that helps you reduce notification overload by letting you choose which apps deliver notifications instantly ("Priority") and which apps are grouped into scheduled summaries ("Batched").

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Notification Listener Permission

Calm Notifications requires Android's Notification Listener permission to provide its core features.

When this permission is enabled, the app reads only:

  • The app name (for example, "WhatsApp")
  • The app's package name

The app does not read, store, or send the content of your notifications. This includes notification titles, messages, text, images, or attachments.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. This includes your Priority and Batched app settings, and notification counts and related preferences.

Calm Notifications does not have a backend server, does not create user accounts, and does not upload your data to the internet.

Advertising

Calm Notifications may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and other information as described in Google's own Privacy Policy to provide, measure, and improve advertisements.

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy to learn more about how Google processes this information.

Data Sharing

Calm Notifications does not sell your personal data, and does not share your locally stored notification settings or statistics with third parties.

Your Choices

You can stop the app from accessing notifications at any time by disabling the Notification Listener permission in your Android settings. You may also uninstall the app whenever you choose.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this same URL.
